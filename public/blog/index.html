Hello World!

Posted on 10th of October 2013

Hello, My name is Daniel Chabr. I am student living in Czech republic and two months ago I decided to build service for all people reading books.

I plan to blog here about all ups and downs of developing a web app project. Some people might call it a startup, but having almost no budget, building it alone and at the same time studying two universities and going to part time job, I think it would be to ambitious to call it that. Let's just call it a project. Nevertheless I do enjoy building this project and I do see value that it might bring, thus I continue building.
